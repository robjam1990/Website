About

Psychosis is a text-based adventure game engine that allows you to create and explore interactive worlds. With a focus on storytelling and player choice, you can shape your character's destiny as you navigate through Thear.

Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or new to the genre, Psychosis offers a rich and immersive experience. From tactical combat to nation building, you'll encounter a wide range of challenges and opportunities as you explore the solar system and interact with its inhabitants.

Are you ready to embark on an epic journey through Thear? Download Psychosis today and begin your adventure.

Features

Psychosis offers a wide range of features to enhance your gaming experience. From state management to tactical combat, you'll find everything you need to create and explore interactive worlds.

Menu

State Management

Manage your character's state seamlessly as you navigate through Thear.

Exploration

Discover every corner of the solar system and explore round planets.

Tactical Combat

Engage in strategic battles with a limb removal system.

Ecosystem Simulation

Interact with a dynamic ecosystem, including animal communication.

Nation Building

Raise a nation to power and join a multi-faction war, managing logistics, agriculture, commerce, and succession.

Social Infrastructure

Experience a fully realized social structure with a bounty system, hierarchies, and individual loyalty, fear, respect, and morality.

Territory Expansion

Expand your territory's borders through strategic decisions and conquest.

Time and Seasons

Experience realistic time, day/night, and seasonal cycles.

Installation

To install Psychosis, use the following command:

npm install psychosis
